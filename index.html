About Our Mission


Shakti is more than just an app—it is a revolution in women's safety. Built with the power of artificial intelligence, Shakti is designed to empower, protect, and support women in situations where they feel unsafe or vulnerable. In a world where harassment and stalking often go unreported due to lack of evidence or fear, Shakti bridges the gap between safety, awareness, and legal action

Our mission is to ensure that every woman feels safe, informed, and in control of her security. Safety is not a privilege—it is a right. Shakti is here to make sure every woman has it.

Key Safety Features

Detects Stalking Pattern

AI analyzes location logs and ride-hailing history to alert you if someone is repeatedly following you.


Warns You About Repeat Offenders

Get real-time risk alerts about individuals with a history of harassment in ride-hailing apps, dating platforms, or public spaces.

Creates Legal Documents for You

Upload messages and AI extracts legal violations to generate court-ready evidence.
